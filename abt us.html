About us



The narrative we're about to share occurs in 2018, on the Tondo neighborhood of Asuncion, where our shop is located. We provide our guests with freshly ground coffee, tea, meals, and sweets. The dream of a corporate employee has now become our reality. We work hard to uphold the greatest standards of quality and service, fusing innovation with tradition to provide our customers with one-of-a-kind experiences. Every coffee cup we offer has a backstory.


At 1198 Asuncion Street in Tondo, Manila, there is a coffee establishment called Leo's Café. The café, which opened on August 9th, 2018, serves a range of hot and cold beverages, such as coffee and smoothies, as well as various food items like burgers, carbonara, and desserts. The café's owner is Sir Leo Richmond Arguelles. The salted caramel latte is a product that Sir Leo suggests because of its distinct sweet and salty flavor.
