About us



The narrative we're about to share occurs in 2018, on the Tondo neighborhood of Asuncion, where our shop is located. We provide our guests with freshly ground coffee, tea, meals, and sweets. What was once a corporate employee's fantasy is now a reality for us. We work hard to uphold the greatest standards of quality and service, fusing innovation with tradition to provide our customers with one-of-a-kind experiences. Every coffee cup we offer has a backstory.


Leo’s Café is a coffee shop located at 1198 Asuncion St. Tondo, Manila. The café was established on August 9, 2018, and offers a variety of hot and cold beverages, including coffee and smoothies, as well as some food items such as burgers, carbonara, and desserts. The owner of the café is Sir Leo Richmond Arguelles. A product that Sir Leo recommends is the salted caramel latte, which is known for its unique sweet and salty taste.
